Background

St. Joseph of Nazareth High School is a leading mixed boarding secondary school located in Mpigi, Uganda. The institution boasts of ultra-modern facilities that provide a conducive learning environment for students. From the moment you set foot in the school, you can feel the sense of excellence and commitment to service that emanates from the staff.

The competent teaching and non-teaching staff at St. Joseph of Nazareth High School are dedicated to ensuring that students receive quality education. The school offers a rich curriculum that is blended with Godly values and extra-curricular activities. This approach to education helps to groom all-round individuals who are capable, analytical, constructive, and independent thinkers..

The school’s commitment to providing quality education extends beyond the classroom. St. Joseph of Nazareth High School encourages engagement in co-curricular activities, which helps to develop students’ talents and interests. This approach to education is grounded in the belief that every journey in this world begins with a step, and at St. Joseph of Nazareth High School, integrity, commitment, faithfulness, and excellence are core values that guide the journey..

It’s no surprise that St. Joseph of Nazareth High School was ranked number one in Mpigi. With first-class facilities and a committed staff, the school is destined for success. The institution aims to provide quality education in a caring environment that will develop God-fearing, self-worthy, academically sound, and responsible citizens and future leaders. At St. Joseph of Nazareth High School, the future of Uganda’s youth is being shaped..
